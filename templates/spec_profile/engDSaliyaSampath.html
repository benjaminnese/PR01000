Professional Bio

Mr. D. S. Sampath received BSc Eng (Hons) degree from University of Peradeniya in 2012 and M.Phil. degree from same university on 2015. He served as an irrigation Engineer attached to the Central Engineering Consultancy Bureau (CECB) for a period of 5 months. Later he joined the Department of Civil Engineering, University of Peradeniya as a Research Assistant for a period of 2 years. Currently, He is affiliated to the Department of Civil Engineering, University of Jaffna as lecturer from Dec 2014 onward. He is serving as a project secretary for WaSo project of University of Jaffna and Water Harmony Erasmus + Project of University of Jaffna from May 2015 onwards. He published 5 journal papers, 15 international conference papers and 7 national conference papers.
