Professional Bio

Prof. S.B. Weerakoon holds a BScEng (First Class) from the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka (1982) as well as an MEng and PhD from the University of Tokyo, Japan (1990). He is currently Senior Professor at the Department of Civil Engineering. He is also a Chartered Engineer and a Fellow of the Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka. His research interests are in the areas of water resources development and management, hydraulic and hydrologic modeling and climate change impact downscaling.
