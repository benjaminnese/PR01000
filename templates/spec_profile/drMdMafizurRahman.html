Professional Bio

Dr. Md. Mafizur Rahman received his Bachelors and Masters degree in Civil Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET) and Ph.D. from University of Tokyo, Japan. He is involved in several national and international projects as a consultant to ADB, UNICEF, NORAD, JICA, World Bank. Since 2013 he is working as a Visiting Professor, University of Tokyo, Japan. He was awarded The University BLUE (BUET) in 1994 for the overall excellence both in the academic arena as well as in Games and athletics. He is the author of the book “Climate change pattern in Bangladesh & Impact on Water Cycle” published by ITN-BUET. His major research interest includes Climate Change impacts on food security, Climate change and water environment, Alternate water resources in urban and rural areas of developing countries, Groundwater pollution and Recharge, Environmental and Water Resources Engineering with GIS application, Urban air pollution, and impact of climate change.
